# 未听清对方话语表述

没有听清楚对方说的话，‌强调的是听觉上的缺失。‌

Sorry, I didn't catch you.

Sorry, I didn't catch that.

Could you speak slowly please?

Could you speak slowly a little?

Could you speak more slowly?

Could you speak up please?

Could you speak up a little?

Could you repeat that, please?

Could you repeat it, please?

Could you mind saying that again?

I beg your pardon?

听到了对方说的话，‌但不明白具体的意思，需要对方进一步解释或说明。

Sorry, I didn't follow you.

Sorry, I didn't get what you said.

Sorry, I can't hear you very well.

Sorry, I didn't understand.

Sorry, I don't know what you mean.

Could you explain more for me?

Could you explain it for more details?

Could you be more specific?

Could you give me a concrete example?

Could you please tell me what you mean by ...

感谢对方的理解。

Thanks for your understanding.

感谢对方的解释。

Thanks for clarifying. I understand better now.
